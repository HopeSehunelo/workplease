The Correlation of Rameses and Pharoah

Rameses II, also known as Ramses II, was a powerful and renowned pharaoh of ancient Egypt.

In the biblical book of Exodus, Pharaoh is the ruler of Egypt who refuses to let the Israelites go.

About the Project

This project explores the correlation between Rameses II and the biblical Pharaoh.

It delves into the historical and biblical accounts of these two figures.
